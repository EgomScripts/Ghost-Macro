This is a 1.8.9 Mod for Hypixel Skyblock and it's purpose
is to be able to farm Ghosts in the Dwarven Mines without
making inputs on your PC so you can be completely AFK and still grind combat xp and sorrows

It is required you have a soul whip and highly recommended 
that you can 2-3 shot ghosts and survive more than a couple hits,
if you cant grind sorrows manually then you wont be able to grind them with this mod
